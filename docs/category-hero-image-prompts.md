# Category Hero Image Prompts

Style baseline (include in every prompt): clean modern lifestyle interior photography,
professional real-estate/product photography lighting, neutral wall tones (soft grey, warm
beige, or cream), one large window as the central subject filling most of the frame, warm
light wood flooring, minimal styled furniture in soft focus at the edges (sofa corner, side
table, small potted plant, framed wall art), natural daylight, shot on a full-frame camera
with a 35mm lens, shallow depth of field, photorealistic, no text, no logos, no watermarks,
horizontal 16:9 landscape composition (crops to the right ~55% of frame on desktop, so keep
the window/product centered-to-right).

Negative prompt (all): text, watermark, logo, illustration, cartoon, 3d render, blurry, low
quality, distorted window frame, people, oversaturated colors

---
